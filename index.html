Design Guidelines

Colors

  • Structural elements are bluey #7788ff or almond #ffaa99.
  • Standard buttons and text are african-violet #cc88ff.
  • More important things are gold #ffaa00.
  • The most important stuff is red #dd4444.
  • If necessary mars #ff2200 can be used for very critical information. Use sparingly if at all!
